## ğŸ“ Managing Blog Posts
### 1. Create a New Post
- Click on **Posts** in the sidebar.
- Click the **+ New Post** button.
- Fill in the **Title**, **Excerpt** (short summary), and **Content**.
- **Visual Editor**: Use the toolbar to make text **bold**, *italic*, or add lists and headings. No HTML needed!
- **Images**: Paste a link to an image in the "Image URL" field.
- **Publish**: Toggle "Publish immediately" to make it live instantly, or leave it off to save as a draft.
- Click **Create Post**.

### 2. Edit an Existing Post
- Click on **Posts** in the sidebar.
- Find your post and click the **Edit** icon (pencil).
- Make your changes and click **Update Post**.
